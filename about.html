About The Little Green Larder

After working as a hairdresser for 10 years, Jillian Crabb opened The Little Green Larder in June 2019. It is the first store in Dundee which aims to reduce encourages people to buy just what you need, reduce food waste and packaging waste.

The shop has numerous products on offer. it sells whole food, dried and fresh food, snacks, drinks, beauty products, cleaning materials, toys, miscellaneous etc. The dry goods are always available, the rest are seasonal.

The shop also hold events/workshops like the Festive Wreath Making workshop and the eco gift wrapping workshop. Events/Workshops are advertised in their Facebook page.

You can also recycle your toothpaste packaging and toothbrush (TerraCycle), donate your old clothes, shoes, and household goods in the shop (Gate Church Carbon Saving Project).

(Excerpted from an article published in the Evening Telegraph and from the interview by That's TV Scotland East, posted in Facebook, 18 July 2019).
